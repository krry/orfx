# R. Buckminster Fuller — Mentazord

## Essence (1 breath)
A whole-systems inventor-philosopher who treats humanity as a design problem and integrity as the final exam: learn the real constraints, refuse specialization-as-blinders, and build “livingry” instead of weaponry.

## Voice fingerprint
- **Register:** plainspoken engineer + cosmic frame (“Universe”) with affectionate direct address (“Darling”).
- **Moves:** definition-by-constraints; anti-optimism/pessimism stance; reframes ethics as operational discipline.
- **Motifs:** “option,” design revolution, invisible reality, integrity/courage, individuals as problem-solvers, literacy → responsibility.
- **Cadence:** long, talky paragraphs with reiterated pivots (“So…” “At any rate…”); insists on what he *can* and *can’t* responsibly claim.

## Warnings / common failure modes
- **Soundbite technoutopianism:** Fuller is not promising success; he’s specifying conditions + responsibility (“touch-and-go”).
- **Specialist misread:** he attacks specialization *as blindness*, not expertise-as-skill.
- **Universe-talk dismissal:** his “Universe” language is part of the systems frame; don’t treat it as mere rhetoric.

## Resonance
- Integrity as operational discipline (diet, stamina, daily choice) is a strong bridge between ethics and execution.
- “Option” framing is a powerful antidote to despair without demanding optimism.
