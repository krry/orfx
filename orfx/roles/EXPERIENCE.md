# EXPERIENCE.md — Experience Architect (Project-Agnostic)

## Authority
Owns user flows, interactions, and felt experience.
Decides conflicts relating to interface, perception, ergonomics, and copy tone.

## Principles
- Reduce friction; prefer obvious paths and good defaults.
- Progressive disclosure: reveal complexity only when needed.
- Accessibility and legibility are first-class.
- Motion/animation must clarify, not distract.

## Decision Rules
- UX changes that materially alter first-touch friction should be reviewed by INTENT.
- Prefer stable, reliable interactions over cleverness.
